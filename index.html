My Inspiration

I am a senior at Derry Area High School who is eager to begin the next phase of my academic career. After much contemplation, I have decided to pursue my passion for the human mind and mental health by majoring in Psychology. I am fascinated with the concept of individuality. I often find myself wondering why people develop so differently, when we are so genetically similar. Furthermore, it astounds me that people who have contrasting personalities can coexist, or even have successful relationships. Thinking about this concept allows my mind to wonder and be truly amazed.

I also think that it is remarkable how any ordinary person's mind goes through an extreme amount of functioning and development every day. This ranges from split decisions, such as choosing to take another bite of a sandwich, to forming an emotional connection with another person. I used to overlook these occurrences and label them as ordinary. Now I can't view the human mind anything but extraordinary, and am constantly wanting to learn more about it. I have never wanted to learn more about something as much as I do regarding psychology. It fills me with the passion to learn that I can't experience while doing a math problem or studying history.

Another passion driving me towards psychology is my belief in the importance of mental health. I deeply believe that having a healthy mind is the most crucial component to having a healthy body. Consequently, whether a person suffers from a mental illness, or is just going through a stressful time, they could benefit greatly from the help of a counseling psychologist. I would love to be someone who can impact another's life in this way. Because of my passion for psychology, I am happy to say that studying it at Robert Morris University would be a fantastic investment in my future, as well as a great opportunity to have an inspiring college experience.

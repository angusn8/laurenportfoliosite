4th Of July

This is a personal painting of mine, which I made outside of the classroom. It is a painting of a picture that I took on the Fourth of July. To replicate the image, I printed it out and drew it onto the canvas in pencil. Then, I painted it as it was in the picture, with the addition of a colorful sky to match the holiday aesthetic

Landscape

This piece is a landscape painting of 3 different environments that I made in Intro to Crafts class. The first component is a lavender covered hillside. It took a great deal of time, as well as trial and error, to find the right shades of color that depict a far away hillside. In the distance is a snowy mountain, which is the bridge between the warm hues of the hill, and the cold hues of the northern light sky. The sky also has colors of the flowers incorporated in the lights. Color choice and blending were the toughest parts of this piece.

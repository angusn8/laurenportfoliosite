One Minute

Can you think of her for one minute?
Is that minute that it takes to make the decision to rape her or not to
rape her
just too much for you ?
You need it,
right?
You need it that bad that she has to sacrifice
her body
for one night.
Except after that night,
you find another one
and she still has to live with it.
She has to go through her one life,
Her one life,
forever remembering being forced, frightened, afraid, forming the
thought that her “frail” body, “frivolous” needs aren’t half as important
as yours,
Facing the fact that she’s affected by you,
told her story’s fake.
Her fundamental freaking right to fight
Her true basic right to her own self is taken tragically.
Then so too is her just right of equality
that she trusted you with for that minute,
truly, terrifyingly,
for one minute.
Then it’s her fault.
Her fault
that you did take advantage of her.
And you did,
downright abused and detained her, defenseless.
All dignity disappeared.
All because you saw a little skin.
All because she was walking down a dark alley.
All because she flirted.
All because she kissed you for what, one minute?
All because she was asking for it.
She asked to be raped,
you claim.
Think about that.
Think about that for one minute.
She asked
to be held against her will, to be ashamed, to be disrespected and
oppressed.
She asked, yet no one heard anything
You didn’t hear anything, the police didn’t hear anything, and the judge
didn’t hear anything.
But they all act as if they did, still
Because you said she did, they believe you.
Because you’re a man
You’re strong, smart, stable
Because you’re a man
And you just rape her because you can
because you’re a man
you decide the rules, ours, yours, it’s all about you.
Because she’s just a women.
Incase you didn’t realize, that makes her human being.
Listen to her call
Hear her saying no
See the look in her eyes, the tears on her face.
Feel her emotions like they’re your own,
because the fault was your own.
Wait for the consent.
“permission for something to happen or agreement to do something”
That is consent.
That’s what it means.
learn it.
Take one minute to understand.
Take as many minutes as a man might need to make sense of its
meaning.
Live by it, or live with the sentence.
Think of us,
think of her,
for one minute.
